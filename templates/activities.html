🚀 Defence Technology Department Activities

Visit to VRDE – DRDO Laboratory

Students of the M.Tech Defence Technology programme visited the Vehicle Research & Development Establishment (VRDE), a premier laboratory under the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), located in Ahmednagar, Maharashtra.

The visit was organized as part of the laboratory assignment to provide practical exposure to defence research and military vehicle technologies. Students learned about the design, development, and testing of advanced defence vehicles used by the Indian Armed Forces.

During the visit, the students observed various testing facilities and gained insights into mobility trials, vehicle engineering, and the research process followed in defence laboratories.

Date: 2026
Location: VRDE, Ahmednagar
Organized by: Department of Defence Technology, VJTI
